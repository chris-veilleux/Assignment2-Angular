In this Angular application, we attempted to recreate a "Ticket Master" like site that exclusively does sports.

This site is intended to be one that a site administrator would use to manage the content on the public site.

On the left navigation bar, you can add and check all records for these five categories!

From the list of records, one can also edit and delete any record already in the table:

Sports

Any sport that the site has access to tickets for.

Games

Any games that would have tickets sold for.

Stadiums

Any professional sports stadium that a team would play in. Good for customers who want info on locations and such.

Credit Cards

Any different credit cards that are allowed for use on the site's e-commerce sales.

Promotions

Any promotion for the sales of the tickets.
